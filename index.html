Our Story

How a family recipe became a neighbourhood institution.

From one recipe to a community cornerstone.

It started in Sandra's kitchen in 2017. She'd been perfecting her grandmother's sourdough recipe for years — a long-fermented loaf with a crackling crust and an open, chewy crumb that no supermarket bread could touch.

When friends and neighbours kept asking where they could buy it, Tom suggested something bold: "Let's open a bakery." Six months later, the keys to a tiny unit on Sopping center were in their hands.

Today, Sandra's Bakery produces over 40 products daily — but every single one is still made by hand, with the same care and the same locally sourced ingredients that went into that first loaf.

What We Stand For

Our values

🌾

Local First

We source our flour, eggs, dairy, and seasonal ingredients from farms within 50 miles. We know our farmers by name.

⏳

No Shortcuts

Our sourdough ferments for 24–48 hours. Our croissant dough is laminated over three days. Good things take time.

🤝

Community

We donate unsold bread to local food banks every evening and partner with the neighbourhood school for baking workshops.
